# Data Classification Matrix

Source of truth: `supabase/migrations/0001`–`0030` (53 tables, verified by direct
grep of `create table public.*`, 2026-06-26). Sensitivity scale: **Restricted**
(legal/financial/safety-sensitive personal data), **Confidential** (tenant-internal
or customer-personal but not top-tier), **Internal** (operational, not personal).

| Data class | Table(s) | Owner | Sensitivity | Tenant scope | Who can access | Who must never access | Retention expectation | Deletion/export | Privacy risk | Required controls |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Business data | `businesses`, `branches` | Business owner | Internal | Per-business | Business members (role-gated for mutation); platform admins (read, via `admin_*` RPCs) | Other businesses; customers (beyond public profile fields shown in-app) | Lifetime of account + grace period | Soft-delete via `deleted_at`; full export on request | Low (mostly operational) | RLS via `is_business_member()` |
| Branch data | `branches` | Business owner | Internal | Per-business | Business members | Other businesses; customers | Lifetime of account | Cascades with business deletion | Low | RLS scoped to `business_id` |
| Staff profile data | `profiles`, `business_members`, `business_invitations` | Individual staff member | Confidential | Per-business membership; `profiles` is platform-wide (1 row per `auth.users` id) | The staff member themselves; business owner/managers for their own business's members; platform admins | Other businesses' staff; customers | Lifetime of account | Soft-delete/removal from `business_members`; `profiles` row persists while `auth.users` exists | Medium (names, role, invite email) | RLS on `business_members`/`business_invitations`; `profiles_update_self` self-only write policy |
| Customer personal data | `customers` | Customer | **Restricted** | Per-business (a person can be a customer of multiple businesses, one `customers` row each) | Business members of that business; the customer themselves (`app_user_id = auth.uid()`) | Other businesses; other customers | Lifetime of relationship + legal/accounting minimum (see retention plan) | Soft-delete via `deleted_at`; export required for data-portability requests | High — name, contact info, linked to vehicles/financials | RLS via `is_business_member()` OR `is_customer_for_business()` |
| Vehicle data | `vehicles`, `projects` | Customer (vehicle owner) | **Restricted** | Per-business + per-customer | Business members; the owning customer | Other businesses/customers | Lifetime of relationship | Cascades with customer deletion (`on delete cascade`) | High — VIN/plate are quasi-identifiers (see next row) | RLS dual-path business/customer |
| VIN / plate / service history | `vehicles.vin`, `vehicles.plate_number`; service history = `jobs`, `quotations`, `vehicle_*` tables joined to a vehicle | Customer | **Restricted** | Per-business + per-customer | Business members; the owning customer | Other businesses/customers; any AI provider call must not forward this beyond what's needed for the lookup | Lifetime of relationship | Same as vehicle data | High — VIN can be cross-referenced outside Revora to identify a specific vehicle/owner | RLS; AI Vehicle Intelligence calls are scoped server-side (see [APPSEC_REVIEW_REPORT.md](APPSEC_REVIEW_REPORT.md)) |
| Quotation data | `quotations`, `quotation_items`, `quotation_revisions` | Business (issuer) / Customer (recipient) | Confidential | Per-business + per-customer | Business members; the linked customer | Other businesses/customers | Per accounting/legal retention (see retention plan) | Soft-delete/lifecycle status; export on request | Medium — pricing, internal notes field (`internal_notes` must stay staff-only) | RLS; verify `internal_notes`-type fields are excluded from any customer-facing query |
| Job/order data | `jobs`, `job_tasks`, `job_updates` | Business / Customer | Confidential | Per-business + per-customer | Business members; the linked customer | Other businesses/customers | Per accounting/legal retention | Soft-delete/lifecycle | Medium | RLS dual-path |
| Contract data | `approvals`, `approval_events`, `terms_versions` (the binding artifact is a quotation + its customer `approvals` signature against a `terms_versions` snapshot) | Business / Customer | **Restricted** | Per-business + per-customer | Business members; the linked customer | Other businesses/customers | Long-term (legal evidentiary value — do not auto-purge without legal input) | Export on request; deletion must preserve legal defensibility (see retention plan) | High — contains signature text, device/user-agent metadata (`approvals.device_data`, `user_agent`) | RLS; treat as a legal record, not routine data |
| Complaint data | `complaints`, `complaint_messages`, `complaint_evidence` | Customer (complainant) / Business (respondent) | **Restricted** | Per-business + per-customer | Business members; the linked customer for their own complaint | Other businesses/customers; **internal staff-only messages must not reach the customer** (per `AGENTS.md`: "Never... Expose internal complaint messages to customers") | Per dispute-resolution/legal retention | Export on request | High — can contain dissatisfaction details, blame allocation, evidence photos | RLS; explicit check that internal-only message flags are enforced server-side, not just hidden in UI |
| Uploaded documents | `documents`, `media_assets`, `vehicle_media_uploads` | Business / Customer (whoever uploaded) | **Restricted** | Per-business, path-prefixed by `business_id` in storage | Business members; linked customer (for evidence they can see); signed-URL holders for the URL's lifetime only | Other businesses/customers; the public (private bucket) | Per document type / legal retention | Deletion must remove both the DB row and the Storage object | High — could include ID photos, signatures, vehicle damage photos | Signed URLs (10 min expiry observed), RLS-backed bucket policies, no public bucket for tenant documents |
| Notification preferences | `notification_preferences`, `business_notification_settings` | Customer / Business | Confidential | Per-business + per-customer | The customer (own prefs); business managers/owners (business settings) | Other businesses/customers | Lifetime of account | Update/delete on request | Low–Medium (reveals contact channel preference) | RLS |
| Notification logs | `notification_events`, `notification_delivery_attempts` | Business | Confidential | Per-business | Business members; service role (dispatch) | Other businesses; customers should not see raw payload/internal status detail | Operational retention (see retention plan) — these can contain `payload` jsonb with personal data | Purge/anonymize on schedule once defined | Medium — payload may embed customer name/contact, delivery provider message IDs | RLS; UUID redaction (`redactNotificationText`) before any customer-facing display |
| Billing/subscription records | `subscriptions`, `subscription_items`, `billing_invoices`, `billing_invoice_items`, `billing_payment_events`, `billing_plans`, `billing_plan_features` | Business | **Restricted** (financial) | Per-business | Business owner/managers; platform admins | Other businesses; customers (these are Revora's bills to the business, not the business's bills to its customers) | Per financial/accounting law (see retention plan; likely 5+ years in UAE — confirm with counsel) | Export on request; do not delete while legally required to retain | High — payment metadata, Stripe identifiers | RLS; Stripe is system of record, webhook-driven, signature-validated |
| AI diagnostic inputs/outputs | `vehicle_symptom_reports`, `vehicle_diagnostic_results`, `vehicle_dtc_codes`, `vehicle_maintenance_plans`, `ai_tool_calls`, `ai_safety_flags` | Customer (subject) / Business | **Restricted** | Per-business + per-vehicle/customer | Business members; the linked customer (their own vehicle's results) | Other businesses/customers; raw model prompts/responses should not be customer-facing if they contain internal tool-call detail | Operational retention (see retention plan) | Export/deletion on request | High — symptom reports may include personal driving/health-adjacent detail; safety flags are sensitive | RLS; safety overrides in `safety.js`; `ai_tool_calls`/`ai_safety_flags` should be staff/platform-only, not customer-readable raw |
| Audit logs | `audit_events` | Platform / Business | **Restricted** | Per-business (nullable on business deletion) | Platform admins; arguably business owners for their own business's events (verify policy) | Other businesses; customers; should never be customer-readable | Long-term (security/legal value) | Should NOT be deleted on user request if it would destroy legally-relevant evidence — flag to legal | High — `old_data`/`new_data` jsonb can contain a full copy of any personal data that changed, plus `ip_address`/`user_agent` | RLS; treat as a security-sensitive system, restrict broad read access even internally |

## Notes and Gaps

- **`audit_events.old_data`/`new_data`** are free-form JSONB snapshots of whatever
  row changed — this means audit logs can contain a copy of *any* other class of
  personal data above. Any retention/deletion policy for "customer personal data"
  must account for its shadow copy in audit logs (see
  [DATA_RETENTION_AND_DELETION_PLAN.md](DATA_RETENTION_AND_DELETION_PLAN.md)).
- **`quotations.internal_notes`** (and similarly-named internal-only fields) must be
  confirmed excluded from every customer-facing query path, not just hidden in the
  UI — this is a query-level control, not a display-level one. Tracked as part of
  [APPSEC_REVIEW_REPORT.md](APPSEC_REVIEW_REPORT.md).
- **VIN and plate number** are treated as Restricted even though they are not
  classic "PII" in some legal frameworks, because combined with a customer record
  they uniquely identify a person's vehicle and movements; treat per UAE/GCC
  guidance once legal review occurs.
- This matrix does not constitute legal classification under any specific privacy
  law (UAE PDPL, GDPR, etc.) — see
  [LEGAL_PRIVACY_REVIEW_CHECKLIST.md](LEGAL_PRIVACY_REVIEW_CHECKLIST.md). Qualified
  counsel must confirm sensitivity tiers and retention periods before relying on
  this document for compliance purposes.
